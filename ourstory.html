Our Story

As a collector, over the years, I have collect several different things ranging from hats to tote bags to records, monies, etc. Of particular interest to me however, are enamel lapel pins and over the years, I have collected a wide variety of pins that tell a story of who I am and the ideals I represent. That represent my tribe. However, as a Nigerian and African in diaspora, I observed that there is no niche market for enamel lapel pins that tell stories or represent ideals of where I come from and the things I grew up seeing and holding dear. For this reason, I have decided to venture out and create that niche market.

Please allow me to introduce to you, Pins and Tribes; an enamel pins production small business committed to producing enamel pins that represent my origin and the tribes I belong to; the ideals I represent and symbols that I hold dear. We are going to be producing pins that represent our food, music, clothing, occupation and even leisure. Categories also include celebrities, icons, and famous people, arts & culture and plants, from my own personal love for nature. Have a look through our gallery, find a tribe and pin it.
